Who am I


My name is Ahmad, I am often asked if I am a professional photographer; my answer is always no!
But, if the opportunity to take pictures professionally arises I will take it in a heartbeat!
My main focus is nature, you will never catch me without my camera if I am adventuring or exploring.
I also enjoy taking pictures that aren't posed, although I have nothing against taking portraits.

I would say that photography is a hobby, as I do it solely because it makes me happy.
When taking photographs you really start to see the beauty in everything,
as well as, realizing that each picture tells a story.

As you can see photography is my passion, and I pride myself on getting the best shot possible.
A lot of people tend to feel insecure when it comes to taking or showing pictures, as you are your worst critic!
Therefore, I tend to post quite a few of my photos on social media, as the inspirational comments from others help me recognize how beautiful each photo actually is.
